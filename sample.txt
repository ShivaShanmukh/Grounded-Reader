EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT — SUMMARY OF KEY TERMS

1. Position and Start Date
The Employee is engaged as a Senior Analyst, commencing on 1 March 2025.

2. Compensation
The annual salary is GBP 62,000, paid monthly in arrears. The Employee is
eligible for a discretionary annual bonus of up to 10% of base salary.

3. Working Hours
Standard working hours are 37.5 hours per week, Monday to Friday.

4. Notice Period
Either party may terminate this agreement by giving thirty (30) days' written
notice. During any probationary period, the notice period is one (1) week.

5. Confidentiality
The Employee shall not disclose any confidential information belonging to the
Company, both during and after the term of employment.

6. Governing Law
This agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales.
